How to Play My Little Farm

  1. Use the shovel to prepare a pot for planting.
  2. Select a seed and click on a prepared pot to plant.
  3. Water your plants regularly using the water can.
  4. Use plant food to boost growth and yield.
  5. Harvest plants when they're fully grown.
  6. Watch out for weather events!

Grow as much as you can before the season ends. Good luck, farmer!
